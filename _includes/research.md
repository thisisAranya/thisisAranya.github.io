### Undergraduate Thesis

<p class="research-title">
  <autocolor><strong>Development of a Multimodal Medical Assistance Chatbot for Domain-Specific Applications</strong></autocolor>
</p>

<p>
  <autocolor>Supervised by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&amp;user=DJUPchAAAAAJ&amp;view_op=list_works&amp;sortby=pubdate">Prof. Mohammad Ariful Haque</a>, with collaborators Tanvir Ahmed Khan and Ismam Nur Swapnil.</autocolor>
</p>

<p>
  <autocolor>Developed a dermatology-focused multimodal medical assistant by fine-tuning a vision-language model on DermNet, using GRPO and DPO for reasoning and conversational alignment, integrating DINOv2 and knowledge-graph-based RAG for diagnostic precision, and applying structured pruning for efficient deployment.</autocolor>
</p>
