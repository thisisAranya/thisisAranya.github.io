I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the [Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering](https://ece.umd.edu/) at the **University of Maryland, College Park**, where I will be advised by [Prof. Sanghamitra Dutta](https://sites.google.com/site/sanghamitraweb/). I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the **Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)**, where my undergraduate research focused on developing Vision Language Models for dermatological assistance, under the supervision of [Prof. Mohammad Ariful Haque](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DJUPchAAAAAJ&hl=en).

I currently work as a **Machine Learning Engineer** at Advanced Chemical Industries Limited, where I build AI systems for defect detection and document analysis. I also served as an **Adjunct Lecturer** at BUET's Department of EEE.

My research lies at the intersection of **efficient AI** and **trustworthy machine learning**, with a special focus on building reliable, resource-aware systems. My interests include **model compression**, **robustness and fairness**, **information-theoretic learning theory**, **vision-language models**, and **medical AI**.
