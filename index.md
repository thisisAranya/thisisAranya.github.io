## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the **University of Maryland, College Park**, advised by [Dr. Sanghamitra Dutta](https://www.sanghamitradutta.com/). Prior to this, I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the **Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)** with a CGPA of 3.87/4.0, where my thesis focused on developing a Vision Language Model for dermatological assistance under the supervision of [Dr. Mohammad Ariful Haque](https://eee.buet.ac.bd/people/faculty/mah).

I also work as a **Machine Learning Engineer** at Advanced Chemical Industries Limited and as an **Adjunct Lecturer** at BUET's Department of EEE.

## Research Interests

- **Efficient AI:** model compression, efficient inference, lightweight architectures
- **Trustworthy AI:** robustness, fairness, interpretability
- **Theoretical ML:** information theory, learning theory
- **Multimodal Learning:** vision-language models, medical AI
- **Reinforcement Learning:** decision-making, sequential learning
